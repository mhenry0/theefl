Pricing


This prices are applicable to individual and groups of maximum 5 students. If you are a company, feel free to contact us at the email thelanguagelab@affiliates.com

Basic / Elementary Level A1 and A2

The individual price is $30 the hour, or its equivalent in colones. For groups it is $15 per person. In groups of several students (not private lessons) the price per person per hour is $10.

Intermediate Level B1 and B2

The individual price is $45 the hour, or its equivalent in colones. For groups it is $20 per person. In groups of several students (not private lessons) the price per person per hour is $10.

Advanced Level C1 and C2

The individual price is $50 the hour, or its equivalent in colones. For groups it is $25 per person. In groups of several students (not private lessons) the price per person per hour is $10.
